KIDS AND TEENS TALENT AFRICA

is a platform set up specially for young, talented and gifted youngsters all over Africa. We want to raise generations of excellent young Africans who will shine their lights the brightest, thereby inspiring other African children to shine as well...until were a continent filled, not only with natural resources but with the best human resources in the world.

We scout for talent in music, acting, poetry, spoken word, comedy, playing of instruments, runway models and many more.

This vision started many years ago in a classroom in Lagos Nigeria. A young teacher decided that the best students weren't only those who excelled in subjects but also those who excelled in non academic fields as well. The goal is to raise influencers who will impact the world positively. Africa, has for too long, been on the back burner. Now is our time to take our rightful position in world affairs and lead, not from the back but from the front.

We have both physical and online contests which have all been very successful. Our physical contest started off with auditions but due to the pandemic, we had to postpone till a layer time, such as when people feel safe enough to gather.

The founder of Kids & Teens Talent Africa is Kola Lenson. He is a school teacher and a foremost believer in the natural abilities of children. He's both a talent scout and an entrepreneur.
